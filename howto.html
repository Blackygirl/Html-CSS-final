Chicken Curry

Ingredients for Chicken curry :

  • Coriander, cumin, turmeric, fennel seeds, cinnamon, pepper, ground mustard, ground cloves, cayenne pepper and salt – these spices build incredible flavor in this dish. Together they create a homemade curry blend.
  • Olive oil - used for sautéing.
  • Fresh onion, garlic and ginger - these aromatics add a key depth of flavor.
  • Low-sodium chicken broth - to create a sauce base to coat the chicken.
  • Canned tomatoes - fresh tomatoes will work here too but canned are quicker and easier.
  • Canned tomatoes - fresh tomatoes will work here too but canned are quicker and easier.
  • Cornstarch - used to thicken the sauce slightly.
  • Heavy cream - this makes the sauce deliciously rich and creamy.
  • Chopped cilantro - adds a nice prop of fresh flavor and color.

Preparation

  • Mix spices together
  • Saute onion, garlic and ginger in a skillet.
  • Add broth and tomatoes, simmer.
  • Blend mixture in a blender as directed then return to skillet.
  • Season sauce then add chicken.
  • Simmer chicken in sauce until cooked through.
  • Add cornstarch slurry during last minute to thicken sauce.
  • Stir in cream.
  • Serve over rice garnished with cilantro.

Pulihora

Ingredients For Pulihora

  • 1 cup Rice,1 tbsp Urad dal,1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 4 tbsp Split Channa Dal
  • 4 tbsp Tamarind Pulp,1 tbsp Split Channa Dal
  • 4 tbsp Oil,1 tsp Mustard Seeds,4 tbsp Raw Peanuts
  • 6 Dry Red Chilli,1 sprig Curry leaves,1 tsp Turmeric powder

Preparation

  • Wash rice and dal together and take them in a pressure cooker. Pour 2 ½ to 3 cups of water and
  • pressure cook them for 3 whistle. Switch off the flame.Let the pressure go all by itself, open the pressure cooker and fluff it with a fork.
  • Now lets makes the tamarind paste. Take tamarind pulp and add half cup of water and bring it to a boil. Cook this till the pulp gets cooked and thick and raw smell gets away. If you are using whole tamarind fruit, first soak that in warm water for 15 mins, squeeze to get the pulp out, strain it and continue with the process.
  • Pour this tamarind pulp over the rice and mix well. Now make the seasoning. Heat oil in a kadai. Add all the ingredients given the seasoning list in the given order and fry them on low heat for 2-3 mins till everything is toasted.
  • Pour this seasoning over the rice and mix well. Serve hot with papad and pickle.

Chicken Fry

Ingredients For Chicken fry :

  • Chicken - I used boneless chicken thigh for making chicken fry. Chicken thighs will result in more juicy chicken. But chicken breast can be used as well.
  • Fresh Produce - ginger, garlic, curry leaves are freshly ground and added to chicken fry for flavor.
  • Spices- chilli, coriander, turmeric and garam masala powder adds so much taste and flavor to the chicken.
  • Lemon - I prefer to add extra lemon juice or vinegar to the chicken to get rid of the chicken smell. it also adds a fresh taste to the chicken.
  • Oil - any neutral oil is used. But personally for frying chicken or fish, I like to use coconut oil. It adds so much flavor.

Preparation

  • Take garlic, ginger and curry leaves in a blender
  • Grind to a coarse puree without adding water
  • Take chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, pepper powder, garam masala powder, turmeric powder and salt in a bowl
  • Add the ground garlic and curry leaves puree
  • Add lemon juice or vinegar
  • Add all purpose flour / maida, Add in rice flour,Mix well,Add in water,Make it into a thick paste
  • Add in chicken cubes. this is boneless chicken thighs,Mix well,Deep fry in hot oil,Cook till golden brown.
  • Drain them and Serve

Fish Curry

Ingredients For Fish curry

  • The masala base is made with onions, tomatoes, fennel seeds, spice powder and coconut. These ingredients are stir fried in oil and ground into a paste. This adds thickness to the gravy.
  • like all curries, this fish curry has onion tomato as base. I like to slice my onions for faster cooking. These provide a thickness to the curry and adds a sweet taste.
  • I use kashmiri chilli powder, coriander powder and turmeric in this. Instead of powder spices you can use them in whole. In that case, you can add 5 to 6 dry red chillies and 3 tbsp coriander seeds.
  • adds a refreshing minty aroma to the dish.
  • I used ginger garlic paste, you can add 1 inch piece of chopped ginger and 6 whole garlic cloves.
  • for fish curry you can use any variety of fish. Usually fish with bones still intact adds flavour to the curry. But if you prefer boneless you can use them too.
  • dried tamarind is soaked in water, squeezed to extract the pulp and used in the curry. Tamarind adds sourness so you can add as much as you prefer.

Preparation

  • Heat coconut oil in a claypot. Add in fennel seeds and saute it for a min. It should sizzle and flavour the entire oil.
  • Add in ginger garlic paste and saute till raw smell leaves from it. Instead of ginger and garlic paste you can use chopped ginger and peeled whole garlic cloves.
  • Add in spice powders and saute for a minute. Instead of using spice powder, you can add whole spices like dry red chillies and coriander seeds
  • Now let's make the fish curry. In the same kadai, add in coconut oil and let it heat Add in mustard seeds and crackle it.
  • Pour in the ground coconut masala and mix well. Stir fry in the oil for 5 to 6 minutes and Cook till oil separates.
  • Pour in tamarind extract. For making the extract, take small lime size tamarind in a bowl, cover it with water and let it soak for 10 minutes. Squeeze the tamarind with your hands and extract the pulp.
  • Add enough salt and mix the fish slices,Cook this on low medium heat for 10 minutes.
  • Add in coriander leaves, cover with lid and let it sit for 30 minutes for the flavours to mingle together.

Paneer

Ingredients And Preparation :

  • First, soak 12 to 15 cashews in hot water for 20 to 30 minutes.
  • When the cashews are soaking, roughly chop 2 large tomatoes, 1 large onion, 1 inch ginger and 6 to 7 small to medium garlic cloves. Crush the ginger and garlic to a paste in mortar-pestle.
  • Add the chopped onions to a blender or grinder jar.
  • Without adding any water, grind or blend to a smooth and fine paste. Remove to a bowl and set aside.
  • To the same grinder jar (no need to wash it), add chopped tomatoes and soaked cashews.
  • Again without adding any water, grind or blend tomatoes and cashews to a fine smooth paste. Keep the jar aside.
  • Heat 3 tablespoons oil in a thick bottomed pan or kadai. Add the onion paste.
  • Sauté the onion paste over a low to medium-low heat, stirring often. Be careful while adding onion paste as it splutters.
  • Sauté till the onion paste begins to caramelize and become golden.
  • Add the crushed ginger-garlic paste. If using ready ginger-garlic paste, then add 1 teaspoon of it.
  • Stir and sauté for few seconds or till the raw aroma of ginger-garlic goes away.
  • Now add the tomato-cashew paste. Be careful as the mixture can splutter.
  • Mix in the cream until well incorporated. Switch off the heat. Taste the cottage cheese curry, adding more salt if required. You can also add ¼ to ½ teaspoon sugar at this step if it tastes too tangy from the tomatoes, being sure to mix it in very well.

Mixed-Vegetable Curry

Ingredients For Vegetable Curry :

  • Vegetable oil or olive oil
  • Veggies — cauliflower, bell pepper, onion, garlic, ginger, jalapeños, green onions, cilantro
  • Seasoning — red curry paste, curry powder, cumin, salt
  • Garbanzo beans
  • Liquids — vegetable broth, tomato sauce, coconut milk

Preparation

  • Saute onion, garlic, ginger, jalapeno.
  • Saute curry paste and spices with it.
  • Mix in 1/4 cup water then puree mixture in food processor.
  • Saute mixture 1 minute.
  • Toss in garbanzo beans, cauliflower, and bell pepper.
  • Stir in vegetable both tomato sauce and coconut milk, Boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 45 minutes.
  • Serve warm over basmati rice, topped with green onions and plenty of cilantro.
